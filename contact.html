Government Panel Admission Checklist

To ensure seamless, 100% cashless check-in for government-funded schemes and subsidies, please prepare the following documents before visiting our administrative office.

1. Patient Identity Proof

National identity cards, passports, or government-issued voter cards that verify date of birth and residency.

2. Scheme Eligibility Card

Active government health panel cards, low-income ration booklets, or authorized social subsidy program documents.

3. Medical History & Referral

Diagnoses, CT scans, previous clinical summaries, or official transfer slips from primary health physicians.

4. Family Boarding Application

(Optional) Income declarations if applying for food and utility grants from the HavenCare Family Compassion Fund.
